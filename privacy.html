Privacy Policy

App: Vanish — Maze Words · Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Vanish app ("we", "the app") handles your information. In short: we do not collect, sell, or share personal data on our own servers. The app relies on Apple services that you control.

Information we do not collect

We do not operate our own accounts or servers. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, or device advertising identifier, and we do not run third‑party analytics or tracking.

Apple services the app uses

Game Center (optional). If you sign in to Game Center, Apple provides leaderboards and achievements. Your Game Center identity and scores are managed by Apple under Apple's privacy policy. We only submit your star totals and achievement progress to Apple's Game Center; we do not store them ourselves.

iCloud (optional). Your game progress (levels, stars, coins, settings, owned items) can be saved to your private iCloud using Apple's Key‑Value Store, tied to your Apple ID. This lets you keep progress across reinstalls and your own devices. This data lives in your iCloud account; we cannot read it.

In‑App Purchases. Purchases (themes, figures, coins, ads‑free) are processed by Apple. We never receive or store your payment details.

Advertising

This version of the app does not display third‑party advertising and does not collect data for advertising. If advertising is added in a future version, this policy will be updated before that feature ships.

Children

Vanish is a family‑friendly word game and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Data retention & deletion

Because we don't hold your data, there is nothing for us to delete. Game Center and iCloud data are managed in your Apple ID settings; deleting the app and its iCloud data removes your saved progress.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
